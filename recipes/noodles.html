Noodles

Description

Noodles, a beloved food enjoyed worldwide, come in a vast of shapes, sizes, and flavors. From delicate rice noodles
to hearty wheat-based varieties, noodles have captured the hearts and stomachs of people across cultures. Whether slurped
in a flavorful broth, tossed in a spicy sauce , or paired with a rich gravy, noodles offer endless culinary possibilities.

Beyond their versatility, noodles often carry cultural significance, representing tradition, comfort, and community.
They can be found in dishes as diverse as Italian pasta, Japanese ramen, and Chinese lo mein, each with its unique history and
preparation techniques. Whether enjoyed as a quick and satisfying meal or a celebratory feast, noodles continue to be a beloved and enduring food.

Ingredients

  • Noodles (ramen, udon, lo mein, etc.)
  • Oyster sauce
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • vegetable
  • protein(beef, fish, chicken etc)

Steps

  1. Boiling noodles
  2. Stir frying noodles
  3. Add vegetable and Protein
  4. Cool and serve
