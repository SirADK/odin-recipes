Fries

Description

Golden-brown and crispy on the outside, with a fluffy, pillowy interior, our French fries are a timeless
classic. Each bite is a symphony of textures and flavors, perfectly seasoned with sprinkle of salt.
Whether you prefer them solo or dipped in your favorite sauce, our fries are the perfect accompaniment to any meal.

Crafted from premium potatoes, our fries are cut to perfection and cooked to golden perfection.
The aroma of sizzling potatoes fills the air, promising a delightful culinary experience.
Each fry is a testament to our commitment to quality and flavor, ensuring a satisfying and unforgettable taste sensation.

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • vegetable oil
  • salt
  • garlic powder
  • black papper

Steps

  1. Prepare the potatoes
  2. Fry the potatoes
  3. Cool and dry
  4. Mix with spices
  5. Final fry
